Only Admin can create accounts.</li><li>No online payment system. Orders are request-based and confirmed by phone.</li><li>Data structure is kept simple: text, images, and basic CRUD operations.</li><li>No enterprise patterns, multi-tenancy, AI integrations, or scalability design.</li><li>Roles: Admin (full access), Content Editor (content only), Farmer (own profile and crops only).</li></ul><p><strong>1.
